Duck Hunt is a game where you have to shoot down ducks I think.

But in this version you have to shoot down circles as they pose a threat to you.

To shoot them down you'll only have to put your mouse above the circle and they'll die and fall.

But be careful as you you need to require bullets, that's where the shop come in!

You can buy bullets with the cash that you receive from shooting the circles down.

Black Circles give you $100
Red Circles give you $115
And Blue Circles give $150

That's all! Click on a gamemode to play! Click the button again to get rid of the instructions.
